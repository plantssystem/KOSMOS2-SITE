KOSMOS2

KOSMOS2 is a generative sound engine designed to create evolving, minimal musical structures. It runs entirely on embedded hardware and operates autonomously without the need for external software or plugins.

The system is built around four interacting components — Melody, Bass, Chord, and Sequence. Each part follows its own internal logic while influencing the others, producing patterns that shift gradually over time.

KOSMOS2 is designed for long-form generative performance, installation work, and minimal electronic composition. Its behavior is deterministic yet never repetitive, allowing it to generate music that feels alive and continuously transforming.
